Astroworld

Astroworld is the third studio album by Travis Scott and it was released on August 3, 2018 through Cactus Jack Records and Grand Hustle Records. The album title is named after the defunct theme park Six Flags Astroworld, which was located in Houston, Texas prior to its closure. Scott, a Houston native, aimed for the album to sound like "taking an amusement park away from kids". Astroworld received widespread accliam and performed well commercially, debuting atop the US Billboard 200 with 537,000 album-equivalent units, of which 270,000 were pure sales.
